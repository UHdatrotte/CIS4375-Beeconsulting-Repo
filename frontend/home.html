About TCI

TCI is a small firm based in Dallas, Texas, with 14 years of excellence in the technology consulting industry. Generating upwards of a million dollars in yearly revenue, we have established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses seeking high-quality technical solutions.

We provide a vast array of services, including custom computer programming, system designs, and API/web service integration. Our practice is focused on delivering projects requiring a high level of technical and functional PeopleSoft expertise, including technical design, development, and functional enhancement for Financials, Supply Chain Management, and HR systems.
